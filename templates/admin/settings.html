Flat Rate

Interest is calculated once on the original principal and spread equally over the loan tenure.

Interest = P × Rate × (Tenure / 12)

Monthly payment is fixed. Good for short-term loans where simplicity is preferred.

Declining Balance (Annual Rate)

Standard loan amortisation. The annual rate ÷ 12 is applied to the outstanding balance each month.

r = Annual Rate / 12 → PMT formula

Most common method. Interest reduces as balance falls. Default for all types.

Declining Balance (Monthly Rate)

The configured rate is treated as a monthly rate (e.g. 2% per month) applied to the declining balance.

r = Rate / 100 (monthly) → PMT formula

Use when the cooperative quotes a per-month rate rather than an annual rate.
